AI Tools & Platforms

ChatGPT

Know More

What is ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a conversational AI developed by OpenAI. It uses a generative pre-trained transformer (GPT) model to understand context, remember previous parts of a conversation, and generate human-like text in response to a wide range of prompts and questions.

Key Features

Natural Conversation: Understands and responds to follow-up questions, admits mistakes, and can reject inappropriate requests.
Content Generation: Drafts emails, writes essays, creates stories, and generates marketing copy.
Coding Assistance: Writes and debugs code snippets in various programming languages.
Multimodal Input: Paid plans can analyze images, voice, and documents (like PDFs and spreadsheets).
Web Browsing: Can access the internet for real-time information on current events.

Plans & Pricing (India)

Free Tier: Provides access to a base model with certain usage limits. Good for casual users and experimentation.
ChatGPT Go: ₹399/month. A budget-friendly plan offering higher message limits, access to GPT-5, and tools for image generation and data analysis.
ChatGPT Plus: ₹1,999/month. Aimed at professionals, this plan gives priority access during peak times, higher usage limits, and access to the most advanced models (like GPT-4o) and features like Sora (text-to-video) and deep research tools.
ChatGPT Pro: ₹19,900/month. For heavy users and professionals requiring unlimited access to GPT-5 and legacy models, along with the highest usage limits and early access to new features.

Common Use Cases

Ideal for students, professionals, developers, and content creators for brainstorming ideas, summarizing long texts, learning new topics, translating languages, and improving productivity.

Gemini

Know More

What is Gemini?

Gemini is a family of multimodal large language models developed by Google AI. It's designed to be "natively multimodal," meaning it can understand, operate across, and combine different types of information seamlessly, including text, code, images, audio, and video.

Key Features

Natively Multimodal: Can process and reason about various data types simultaneously. You can upload an image and ask questions about it in natural language.
Advanced Reasoning: Capable of sophisticated reasoning for complex subjects like math and physics, and can help identify and debug complex code.
Model Tiers: Comes in different sizes for various applications—Ultra (highest capability), Pro (all-around best model), and Nano (for on-device tasks).

Plans & Pricing (India)

Gemini (Free): The standard experience, which uses the powerful Gemini Pro model. It's free and available for general use at gemini.google.com.
Gemini Advanced (Paid): This is part of the Google One AI Premium plan for ₹1,950/month, often including a free 2-month trial. It provides access to Gemini 1.5 Pro, Google's most advanced model, with a massive context window for analyzing large documents, plus integration into Gmail, Docs, Slides, and more.

Ecosystem Integration

Gemini is deeply integrated into the Google ecosystem. You can access its capabilities directly within Google Workspace apps (with the AI Premium plan), on Android devices through the Gemini app, and in various Google Cloud services for developers.

Microsoft Copilot

Know More

What is Microsoft Copilot?

Microsoft Copilot is an AI-powered intelligent assistant that is deeply integrated into Microsoft's ecosystem. It leverages large language models (like OpenAI's GPT-4) to enhance productivity, creativity, and information access across Windows, the Edge browser, and Microsoft 365 applications.

Key Features

OS Integration: Built into Windows 11 for system-level tasks and quick answers.
Web Intelligence: Integrated into the Edge browser to summarize pages, compose text, and answer questions about web content.
M365 Integration (Pro): Works inside Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams to draft documents, analyze data, design presentations, and summarize meetings.
Image Creation: Generates AI images from text descriptions using Designer (formerly DALL-E 3).

Plans & Pricing (India)

Copilot (Free): Included with Windows, Edge, and available online. Provides standard access to foundational AI models, chat capabilities, and image creation.
Copilot Pro: ₹2,000 per user/month. This premium plan offers priority access to the latest models (like GPT-4 Turbo) for faster performance, enhanced AI image creation, and most importantly, unlocks Copilot features within Microsoft 365 apps (Word, Excel, etc.).
Note: To use Copilot Pro in Office apps, you must have a separate, active Microsoft 365 Personal or Family subscription.

Who Is It For?

Ideal for individuals and businesses heavily invested in the Microsoft ecosystem. The Pro version is particularly powerful for professionals and students who use Microsoft 365 apps daily for work or study.

Perplexity

What is Perplexity?

Perplexity is an AI-powered "answer engine" that functions as a hybrid between a search engine and a chatbot. Instead of just providing a list of links, it directly answers questions by summarizing information from the web and provides citations for its sources, allowing for easy verification.

Key Features

Sourced Answers: Every answer is backed by a list of sources, so you can check where the information came from.
Copilot Mode: An interactive search guide that asks clarifying questions to better understand your query and refine the search results.
Focus Modes: Allows you to narrow your search to specific sources like Academic papers, YouTube, Reddit, or WolframAlpha.
File Uploads: You can upload files (PDFs, TXT) and ask questions about their content.

Plans & Pricing (India)

Free Tier: Offers a generous amount of standard searches and a limited number of Copilot-guided searches per day.
Perplexity Pro: Costs $20/month (approx. ₹1,670) or $200/year (approx. ₹16,700). It offers unlimited Copilot searches, unlimited file uploads, and the ability to choose from advanced AI models like GPT-4o and Claude 3 Opus for more comprehensive answers. (Note: Billed in USD, final INR price may vary based on bank conversion rates).

Who is it for?

Excellent for students, researchers, journalists, and professionals who need quick, verifiable answers with clear sources. It's a powerful tool for research, fact-checking, and learning new topics efficiently.

Grok

Know More

What is Grok?

Grok is a generative AI chatbot developed by Elon Musk's xAI. It's designed to answer questions with a bit of wit and personality, modeled after "The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy." Its key differentiator is its real-time access to information from the X (formerly Twitter) platform.

Key Features

Real-Time Information: Directly integrates with X to provide up-to-the-minute answers on current events and trending topics.
Unique Personality: Known for its less-filtered, sometimes witty, and rebellious responses compared to other AI models.
Multimodal Capabilities: Can process and analyze text, images, and soon, video. Also features text-to-image generation.
Integrated Platform: Built directly into the X platform and app, providing summaries of posts and aiding in search.

Plans & Pricing (India)

Grok is available exclusively through the X Premium+ subscription. There is no separate or standalone subscription for the AI.
X Premium+ (Web): Approx. ₹1,300/month or ₹13,600/year.
X Premium+ (Mobile App): Approx. ₹2,299/month or ₹22,900/year.
Note: Pricing via mobile app stores (Apple App Store, Google Play Store) is higher due to platform fees. Subscribing via the web is more cost-effective.

Common Use Cases

Best suited for users who are active on the X platform and want real-time summaries, unfiltered opinions, or creative and humorous content generation. It's also useful for tracking breaking news and public sentiment on various topics as they unfold.

Claude

Know More

What is Claude?

Claude is a next-generation AI assistant developed by Anthropic. It's built on principles of AI safety and is designed to be helpful, harmless, and honest. It excels at thoughtful dialogue, content creation, and complex reasoning, making it a strong alternative to other LLMs.

Key Features

Large Context Window: Its standout feature. Claude can process huge amounts of text at once (up to 200,000 tokens, equivalent to a 500-page book), making it perfect for summarizing long documents, reports, or codebases.
Higher Accuracy: Often produces fewer instances of "hallucination" or incorrect information compared to some competitors.
Image Analysis: Can analyze and answer questions about images, charts, and diagrams.
Code Generation: Proficient in writing, explaining, and debugging code across multiple languages.

Plans & Pricing (India)

Free Tier: Offers generous access to the Claude 3 Sonnet model on claude.ai. Usage is limited by a messaging cap that resets every few hours.
Claude Pro: Priced at $20 USD per month (plus applicable taxes like GST). This plan provides at least 5 times the usage of the free tier, priority access during high-traffic periods, early access to new features, and the ability to use their most powerful model, Claude 3 Opus.

Common Use Cases

Excellent for professionals and researchers who need to analyze dense legal contracts, financial reports, or scientific papers. Also powerful for creative writing, detailed content editing, and as a collaborative brainstorming partner.

Replit

Know More

What is Replit?

Replit is an online Integrated Development Environment (IDE) that lets you start coding instantly in your browser. It requires zero setup, allowing you to write, run, and share code from any device, making it a powerful tool for rapid development and learning.

Key Features

Browser-Based IDE: Access a full coding environment from anywhere without any installation.
50+ Languages: Instant templates for Python, JavaScript, Java, C++, HTML/CSS, and many more.
Real-time Collaboration: "Multiplayer" mode allows multiple users to code together in the same files.
Integrated AI: Replit AI helps you write, explain, and debug code faster.
Instant Hosting: Deploy websites, APIs, and bots with a single click directly from the editor.
GitHub Integration: Push and pull repositories directly from your workspace.

Plans & Pricing (India)

Free Tier: Includes access to the basic IDE, public projects (Repls), multiplayer collaboration, and limited computational resources. Perfect for students and hobbyists.
Replit Core: Priced at $20/month (approx. ₹1670). Offers private projects, a more powerful machine for faster performance, increased storage and resource limits, and enhanced AI features. This plan is ideal for professional developers and serious builders.

Who is it For?

Excellent for students learning to code, educators creating assignments, developers building prototypes, and teams collaborating on projects. Its simplicity and power make it suitable for both beginners and experienced programmers.

DeepSeek

Know More

What is DeepSeek?

DeepSeek is an AI research initiative that develops high-quality, open-source large language models. Its models are particularly renowned for their advanced capabilities in code generation, mathematics, and logical reasoning, often outperforming larger models in these specific areas.

Key Models & Features

DeepSeek Coder: A specialized model trained on a massive dataset of code, making it highly proficient at writing, completing, and explaining code in many languages.
DeepSeek LLM: A general-purpose model with strong language understanding and generation capabilities for tasks like writing, summarization, and translation.
Open Source Focus: Many of its powerful models are released open-source, allowing developers to self-host and fine-tune them.
Cost-Effective: The API pricing is extremely competitive, making it an affordable choice for businesses and developers.

Platform & Pricing

Web Chat: DeepSeek offers a free-to-use web interface (DeepSeek Chat) for direct interaction with its models, similar to other AI chatbots. Currently, there is no paid "Pro" subscription for the chat interface.
API Access: Developers can access DeepSeek models via a pay-as-you-go API. Pricing is based on token usage and is globally set in USD. It is known for being one of the most affordable options on the market (e.g., prices can be as low as $0.14 USD per million tokens for some models), making it very budget-friendly for Indian developers and businesses.

Who is it for?

Primarily for developers, programmers, researchers, and tech companies needing a powerful and affordable AI model for coding-intensive tasks, data analysis, and as a cost-effective alternative to other proprietary LLMs.

DALL·E

Know More

What is DALL·E?

DALL·E is an AI system developed by OpenAI that can create realistic images and art from a natural language description (text prompt). It can generate original images, modify existing images, and create variations of images.

Key Features

Text-to-Image Generation: Creates diverse images based on detailed text prompts.
Inpainting: Modifies specific areas of an image, adding or replacing elements.
Outpainting: Extends an image beyond its original borders, imagining what could be outside the frame.
Variations: Generates multiple stylistic or content variations of an uploaded image.
Style Transfer: Can create images in various artistic styles, from photorealistic to abstract.

Access & Pricing (India)

Included with ChatGPT Plus/Pro: DALL·E 3 is primarily accessed through ChatGPT Plus (₹1,999/month) or ChatGPT Pro (₹19,900/month) subscriptions, where users can generate images directly within the chat interface.
API Access: For developers, DALL·E is also available via OpenAI's API, with pricing based on image resolution and quantity generated. (e.g., DALL·E 3 pricing can be around $0.04 - $0.08 per image for standard resolutions, subject to change and exact API usage).
Free Access: Limited free access to DALL·E 2 might be available through some third-party integrations or specific OpenAI promotions; however, direct free access to DALL·E 3 is typically bundled with ChatGPT subscriptions.

Best For

Artists, designers, content creators, marketers, and anyone needing quick visual assets or looking to explore creative concepts without extensive graphic design skills.

MidJourney

Know More

What is MidJourney?

MidJourney is a generative AI service that produces images based on natural language descriptions, known as "prompts." It is renowned for creating complex, detailed, and artistically stylized images. Initially accessed via the Discord app, it now also has a dedicated web interface.

Key Features

High-Quality Images: Excels in generating artistic, surreal, and photorealistic images with a distinct style.
Style Reference: Allows users to upload an image to guide the artistic style (colors, textures, mood) of the output.
Character Reference: Maintains character consistency across multiple generated images.
Advanced Parameters: Offers extensive control through parameters for aspect ratio (`--ar`), style (`--s`), and negative prompts (`--no`).
Active Community: Operates through a large Discord community, enabling users to see others' creations and prompts.

Plans & Pricing (India - Approx.)

Basic Plan: ~$10/month (approx. ₹830). Provides about 200 image generations ("Fast GPU" minutes). Ideal for new users.
Standard Plan: ~$30/month (approx. ₹2,500). Includes 15 hours of Fast GPU time and unlimited "Relaxed" generations, which are slower.
Pro Plan: ~$60/month (approx. ₹5,000). Offers 30 hours of Fast GPU time, unlimited Relaxed generations, and "Stealth Mode" to keep images private.
Mega Plan: ~$120/month (approx. ₹10,000). For heavy users, providing 60 hours of Fast GPU time and all Pro features.

Common Use Cases

Perfect for artists, designers, and content creators for generating concept art, illustrations, marketing visuals, mood boards, and unique digital art without needing traditional artistic skills.

Wix Studio

What is Wix Studio?

Wix Studio is a flexible, end-to-end web creation platform designed for agencies, freelancers, and enterprises. It combines advanced design features like responsive AI and custom CSS with Wix's robust business solutions, allowing professionals to build exceptional websites with high efficiency.

Key Features for Professionals

Responsive AI: Design smart, fluid layouts that automatically adjust to any screen size with optimal responsiveness.
No-Code Animations: Add complex animations and interactions (like scroll, hover, click) without writing any code.
Headless CMS: Use Wix's powerful content management system as a backend for any front-end technology.
Agency Tools: A centralized workspace to manage multiple sites, clients, and team members, including a reusable asset library.
Velo IDE: Add custom functionality and sophisticated web applications using JavaScript and a built-in development environment.

Pricing Plans (India)

Wix Studio offers premium plans that include professional tools and remove Wix branding. Prices are approximate and cheaper if billed annually.
Standard: ~₹850/month. Ideal for showcasing a professional portfolio or a client's brand.
Plus: ~₹1,250/month. Great for collecting leads and building a substantial online presence.
Elite: ~₹2,500/month. Best for e-commerce, accepting online payments, and advanced business applications.

Who Is It For?

Wix Studio is built for web professionals who need full design control and a scalable platform to deliver high-quality client sites efficiently. It directly competes with platforms like Webflow and is the successor to Editor X.

Boltt

What is Boltt?

Boltt is an Indian consumer electronics brand that designs and markets a wide range of audio and wearable devices. The company is known for its focus on affordability, stylish designs, and integrating modern features into budget-friendly products.

Key Product Categories

Smartwatches: Feature-rich wearables with AMOLED displays, Bluetooth calling, and comprehensive health tracking (SpO2, heart rate, sleep monitoring).

Soundbars & Speakers: Home and portable audio systems designed to deliver powerful sound.

General Price Range

Products typically range from affordable entry-level (approx. ₹800 - ₹1,500) for audio devices to mid-range (approx. ₹2,000 - ₹5,000) for advanced smartwatches and soundbars, offering strong value across their lineup.

Rocket

Know More

What is Rocket?

Rocket is a platform designed for entrepreneurs to launch, manage, and scale their companies. It simplifies the complex process of US company incorporation (typically as a Delaware C-Corp), legal compliance, and initial financial setup, making it accessible to founders worldwide.

Key Features

US Incorporation: Fast, streamlined process to register a company in Delaware, including all necessary legal filings.
Legal Documents: Generates essential post-incorporation documents like Founder Stock Purchase Agreements and Bylaws.
Cap Table Management: Provides tools to issue stock and manage your company's capitalization table from day one.
Startup Perks: Access to over $500,000 in credits and discounts on popular tools like AWS, Mercury, and Stripe.
Banking & EIN: Facilitates opening a US business bank account and obtaining a federal Employer Identification Number (EIN).

Pricing Model

Launch Package: Typically a one-time fee (around $599 USD) that covers the complete incorporation process, legal documents, and lifetime access to the platform and perks. State filing fees are separate and vary by state.
Add-ons: Offers additional services like registered agent services, tax consultations, and compliance management for annual fees.

Who is it for?

Ideal for early-stage tech startups, global founders (especially non-US residents) wanting to establish a US entity, and entrepreneurs planning to raise venture capital from US investors.
