E-Commerce & Fashion

Flipkart

One of India's leading e-commerce marketplaces for a vast range of products.

What is Flipkart?

Flipkart is a premier Indian e-commerce company, offering a massive online marketplace for everything from electronics and fashion to groceries and furniture. It is known for its competitive pricing and expansive reach across the country.

Flipkart Plus Membership

How it Works: Flipkart Plus is a free loyalty program. Users automatically become members after earning 200 SuperCoins (reward points) within a 12-month period.
Benefits: Members get free & fast delivery on 'Flipkart Assured' items, early access to major sale events, superior customer support, and earn bonus SuperCoins on purchases.

Major Sale Events

The Big Billion Days: Flipkart's flagship annual sale, typically held in September or October before Diwali. It offers the biggest discounts of the year.
Big Saving Days: Held multiple times a year, offering significant discounts across various categories.
Other Sales: Look out for sales during major festivals and holidays like Republic Day, Holi, and Independence Day.

What Sells More?

Flipkart is a top destination for smartphones, large appliances (TVs, ACs), and electronics, which see massive sales during discount events. Fashion, home goods, and beauty products are also extremely popular categories on the platform.

Myntra

India's leading e-commerce platform for fashion and lifestyle products.

What Myntra Offers

Myntra is a one-stop shop for fashion and lifestyle, offering a wide range of products including apparel, footwear, accessories (watches, bags), jewelry, personal care products, and home décor. It features a vast collection of national and international brands.

What Sells More

Top Categories: Casual Wear, Ethnic Wear (Sarees, Kurtas), Sportswear, and Western Wear are the highest-selling apparel categories.
Other Hot Sellers: Sneakers, Handbags, Watches, and Beauty & Grooming products are also extremely popular among users.

Major Sale Events

End of Reason Sale (EORS): Myntra's flagship and largest sale event, happens twice a year (June-July & December-January).
Big Fashion Festival: Held around the festive season (September-October) before Dussehra and Diwali.
Other Sales: Look out for the 'Right to Fashion Sale' (around August), 'Myntra's Birthday Sale', and numerous brand-specific or smaller festive sales throughout the year.

Membership Program: Myntra Insider

Type: A free, points-based loyalty program. Users earn 'Insider Points' on every purchase.
Benefits: Early access to major sales, exclusive offers, savings on partner platforms (Zomato, Tata Cliq), and fashion advice from celebrity stylists.

Typical New User Offers

First-time users often receive benefits like a flat discount (e.g., ₹300-500 off on their first order), free shipping, and special coupons. Check the app or website upon signup for the latest offer. The platform generally holds a high rating (4.4+ stars) on app stores.

AJIO

A one-stop online fashion destination by Reliance with curated brands from India and beyond.

What is AJIO?

AJIO is a digital fashion and lifestyle brand from Reliance Retail. It offers a wide collection of clothing, footwear, and accessories, featuring a mix of national, international, and exclusive in-house brands. It's known for its curated style guides and unique sections like AJIO LUXE and Sneakerhood.

What it Offers

Wide Range: Apparel, footwear, accessories, and tech products for men, women, and kids.
Brand Portfolio: Features over 2000 brands, including top international labels and exclusive Indian brands.
Special Curation: 'Indie' section for artisanal products, 'AJIO LUXE' for high-end brands, and 'Sneakerhood' for sneaker enthusiasts.

Major Sale Events

AJIO Mania Sale: One of its biggest sales, occurring multiple times a year, offering massive discounts.
Big Bold Sale: Typically a mid-year event (around June-July) with discounts ranging from 50-90%.
All-Stars Sale: Another major sale event, often preceding the festive season (around September-October).
Festive Sales: Special sales during major festivals like Diwali, Christmas, and New Year.

Membership & Offers

Loyalty Program (AJIO Prive): AJIO doesn't have a flat-fee membership like Amazon Prime. Instead, it runs a points-based loyalty program called AJIO Prive. Benefits are tiered based on purchase history and include early access to sales, exclusive vouchers, and special member-only deals.
New User Offers: First-time users often get a special discount, typically a flat percentage off (e.g., 30% off) or a fixed amount on their first purchase. Check the app/website for the current live offer.

What Sells More?

Popular categories include ethnic wear (kurtas, sarees), trendy western wear (dresses, tops), and a vast collection of sneakers and casual shoes. Brands like Adidas, Nike, Puma, and Levi's are consistent best-sellers.

Nykaa

India's leading online destination for beauty, wellness, and fashion products.

What is Nykaa?

Nykaa is a premier Indian e-commerce platform offering a comprehensive selection of authentic beauty, wellness, and fashion products. It serves as a one-stop-shop for makeup, skincare, haircare, fragrances, and apparel from both Indian and international brands.

Popular Categories & Products

What Sells More: Skincare (serums, moisturizers), Makeup (foundations, lipsticks), and Haircare (shampoos, conditioners) are the highest-selling categories.
Also Offers: A wide range of personal care items, luxury beauty products, and a dedicated fashion vertical, 'Nykaa Fashion', for clothing and accessories.

Major Sale Events

Nykaa hosts several major sales throughout the year with significant discounts. Key Sales: Hot Pink Sale (around July), Pink Friday Sale (November), and seasonal sales during Diwali, Republic Day, and Women's Day.

Membership & Offers

Loyalty Program (Nykaa Prive): This is a free, tier-based loyalty program. Customers get upgraded to 'Prive', 'Gold', and 'Platinum' tiers based on their spending, unlocking benefits like early access to sales, exclusive discounts, and birthday gifts.
New User Offers: Typically provides a discount on the first app or web order, such as "Flat 10% off" or "₹200 off on first purchase above a certain amount."

Overall Rating

Widely regarded as a trusted and reliable platform in India for authentic beauty products, with a massive and loyal customer base. It is a go-to choice for most online beauty shoppers.

Meesho

An Indian e-commerce platform offering a wide range of affordable products, direct from suppliers.

What is Meesho?

Meesho is a popular Indian online marketplace known for its focus on affordability. It connects unbranded suppliers and small manufacturers directly to consumers, aiming to provide a vast selection of products at wholesale prices, especially in Tier-2 and Tier-3 cities.

What Sells More? (Popular Categories)

Fashion: Especially Women's Ethnic Wear (Sarees, Kurtis), Western Wear, and Men's Apparel.
Home & Kitchen: Decor items, kitchenware, bedsheets, and utility products.
Beauty & Health: Makeup, skincare, and personal care items.
Jewellery & Accessories: Fashion jewellery is a very popular and high-selling category.

Offers, Sales & Membership

New User Offers: Typically offers a discount on the first purchase, like "Up to 30% off" or "₹100 off your first order".
Sale Events: Frequent sales occur, with major events named "Mega Blockbuster Sale" or "Maha Indian Savings Sale," especially during festive seasons like Diwali, Holi, and Independence Day.
Membership: Meesho does not have a paid 'Pro' or 'Prime' membership model; all users have access to the same low prices and offers.

Overall Rating & Target Audience

Meesho is widely regarded as a go-to platform for budget-friendly shopping. It's ideal for price-conscious consumers looking for variety in fashion and home goods without prioritizing major brand names.

Nike

A global leader in athletic footwear, apparel, and equipment for sport and lifestyle.

What Nike Offers

Nike is a premier brand for high-performance sports gear and lifestyle fashion. Its offerings include athletic footwear, casual sneakers, apparel like Dri-FIT clothing and Tech Fleece tracksuits, and sports equipment.

Product Range & Best-Sellers

Footwear: Iconic lines include Air Jordan, Air Force 1, Dunk, Air Max, and performance series like Pegasus (running) and LeBron (basketball).
Apparel: From professional jerseys to everyday athleisure wear, hoodies, and t-shirts.
Apps: The Nike Run Club (NRC) for runners and SNKRS for exclusive sneaker releases and "drops."

Nike Membership (Free)

Nike does not have a paid "pro" membership. Instead, it offers a free Nike Membership with significant perks.
Benefits: Exclusive access to new products, special member-only offers, birthday rewards, free standard shipping on all orders, and access to premium features in the Nike Training Club (NTC) and NRC apps.

Sales & Offers (India)

Major Sale Events: Look for End of Season Sales (EOSS) typically in January and July. Big discounts also appear during major Indian festivals like Diwali (Oct-Nov) and Independence Day (Aug).
New User Offers: While there isn't a standard first-time discount, signing up for the Nike newsletter is the best way to receive notifications about promotions and potential coupons.

Puma

A global athletic brand designing and manufacturing footwear, apparel, and accessories.

What Puma Offers

Puma is a leading sports and lifestyle brand offering a wide range of products for performance and athleisure. It is known for its innovative designs that blend sports with street style culture, backed by collaborations with major celebrities and sports teams.

Popular Products & Categories

What Sells More: Sneakers are the top-selling category, especially lifestyle models like the Puma Suede, Rider, RS-X series, and classic motorsport footwear.
Key Categories: Running Shoes, Cricket Gear, Football Kits, Motorsport Collaborations (Ferrari, BMW), Athleisure Wear (T-shirts, Tracksuits, Hoodies), and Accessories (Backpacks, Caps).

Major Sale Events (India)

End of Season Sale (EOSS): The biggest sale event, happening twice a year (typically June-July and Dec-Jan) with discounts up to 60% off.
Festive Sales: Major offers during Diwali, Republic Day, and Independence Day.
Puma Suede Day: Special promotions celebrating their iconic sneaker, usually in November.

Offers & Membership

New User Offer: Typically offers a 10-15% discount on the first purchase after signing up for their newsletter.
Membership: Puma does not have a paid "pro" membership. However, signing up on their website or app provides access to exclusive launches, early access to sales, and personalized offers.

Market Standing

Puma holds a strong position in the Indian market, competing directly with Nike and Adidas. It is highly popular among the youth for its trendy designs and strong brand endorsements from top Indian celebrities like Virat Kohli, KL Rahul, and Kareena Kapoor Khan.

Adidas

A global leader in sportswear, footwear, and accessories for athletes and lifestyle.

What Adidas Offers

Adidas is a German multinational corporation that designs and manufactures high-quality athletic and lifestyle footwear, apparel, and accessories. It's known for its iconic three-stripe logo and a deep heritage in sports and culture.

Product Range & Popular Items

What Sells More: Footwear is a major driver, especially classic sneakers and performance running shoes.
Footwear: Iconic lifestyle sneakers (Superstar, Stan Smith), performance running shoes (Ultraboost), football boots (Predator), and sliders.
Apparel: Tracksuits, jerseys for major football clubs, t-shirts, hoodies, and sports bras.
Accessories: Backpacks, caps, socks, and sports equipment.

adiClub Membership

Membership Fee: The 'adiClub' is a free-to-join loyalty program.
Benefits: Members earn points for purchases and engagement. Tiers (Level 1 to 4) unlock benefits like members-only products, early access to sales, birthday bonuses, priority customer service, and exclusive event invitations.

Sales & Offers (India)

New User Offer: Typically offers a discount (e.g., 15% off) on the first purchase after signing up for their newsletter or adiClub.
Sale Names & Dates: Look out for the "End of Season Sale" (EOSS), usually in June-July and December-January. Adidas also participates heavily in major online festive sales during Diwali (Oct-Nov) and Republic Day (Jan).

Who is it for?

Ideal for athletes, fitness enthusiasts, sneaker collectors ("sneakerheads"), and anyone interested in high-quality athleisure and streetwear fashion.

Snitch

A popular Indian D2C brand offering trendy and affordable fast fashion for men.

What is Snitch?

Snitch is a direct-to-consumer (D2C) men's apparel brand based in India. It is known for its fast-fashion approach, bringing trendy, runway-inspired designs to the market quickly and at affordable prices. The brand gained significant popularity after its appearance on Shark Tank India.

Product Range & Popular Items

Apparel: Offers a wide range including Shirts, T-shirts, Trousers, Jeans, Shorts, Jackets, and Sweaters.
Specialty: Particularly known for its stylish Co-ord sets, printed and party-wear shirts, and oversized fits.
Accessories: Also provides a selection of accessories like perfumes and sunglasses.

Offers & Sales

New User Offers: Typically provides a flat percentage discount (e.g., 10-15% off) on the first order upon signing up for their newsletter.
Sale Events: Hosts sales during major Indian festivals (like Diwali), End of Season Sales (EOSS), and participates in larger shopping events like the Black Friday Sale.
Loyalty Program: Snitch has a point-based loyalty program where customers earn points on purchases, which can be redeemed for discounts on future orders.

Who is it for?

The brand primarily targets fashion-conscious young men (18-35) looking for stylish, modern, and trendy clothing that doesn't break the bank. It's ideal for those who like to keep their wardrobe updated with the latest styles.

Zara

A global fast-fashion brand known for bringing trendy, runway-inspired apparel to the high street.

What is Zara?

Zara is a Spanish apparel retailer specializing in fast fashion. They are famous for their ability to quickly design, produce, and distribute new styles, often replicating the latest high-fashion trends and making them available to the mass market at affordable prices.

What Sells More?

Best-Sellers: Blazers, tailored trousers, seasonal dresses, and coats are consistently popular.
Staples: Their high-quality basics like knitwear, t-shirts, and denim are also strong sellers.
Accessories: Trendy bags, shoes, and belts are key items that sell out quickly.

Main Sale Events (India)

Zara primarily holds two major sale events each year rather than frequent small discounts.
Summer Sale: Typically starts online and in-store in the third or fourth week of June.
Winter Sale: Usually begins right after Christmas, from the 26th of December onwards.
Note: Discounts often increase as the sale progresses, but the most popular items sell out on the very first day.

Offers & Membership Program

New User Offers: Zara generally does not offer specific "new user" discounts or promo codes.
Membership: There is no paid "prime" or loyalty membership. A standard free account allows for easier checkout and order tracking. All customers have equal access to sales and products.

Brand Perception (Overall Rating)

Zara is highly regarded for its on-trend, fashionable designs at an accessible price point. While it's a go-to for stylish and modern apparel, it is positioned as a fast-fashion brand, with quality that is generally considered good for its price but not at a luxury level.

H&M

Global fashion brand offering on-trend clothing, accessories, and home goods for the whole family.

What is H&M?

H&M (Hennes & Mauritz) is a Swedish multinational retail company known for its fast-fashion clothing. It offers a wide range of affordable and trendy apparel, accessories, and, in many locations, home decor items under the H&M Home brand.

Product Range

Apparel: Includes casual wear (t-shirts, jeans), formal wear, dresses, activewear, and outerwear for women, men, and kids.
Accessories: Bags, belts, jewelry, scarves, and footwear.
Special Collections: Known for its "Conscious" collection made from sustainable materials and frequent designer collaborations.
H&M Home: Offers a variety of home goods like cushion covers, bedding, rugs, and decorative items.

H&M Loyalty Program

The program is free to join and has two tiers:
Member: Get 1 point for every ₹1 spent, receive bonus vouchers (e.g., ₹300 off after 1500 points), access to member-only prices, and a birthday offer.
Plus Member: Unlocked after collecting 5000 points. Includes all member benefits plus free shipping on all orders, unique access to special collections, and exclusive event invitations.

Offers & Major Sales in India

New User Offer: Typically offers 10% off the first purchase upon signing up for the Loyalty Program.
End of Season Sales: The biggest sales with up to 70% off, usually occur in June-July (Summer Sale) and December-January (Winter Sale).
Festive Sales: Special offers and collections during major Indian festivals like Diwali and Christmas.
Black Friday Sale: Held in late November with significant discounts across all categories.

Overlays

An Indian streetwear brand for minimalist and high-quality everyday apparel.

What is Overlays?

Overlays is an online Indian fashion brand that focuses on high-quality, minimalist, and comfortable streetwear. They are known for their clean designs, premium fabrics, and focus on oversized and relaxed fits suitable for everyday wear.

Product Range

Tops: A wide variety of oversized T-shirts (both plain and graphic), full-sleeve tees, and shirts.
Outerwear: Hoodies, sweatshirts, and jackets with a focus on comfort and minimalist aesthetics.
Bottoms: Includes joggers, shorts, and cargo pants designed for a relaxed fit.

Offers & Sales

New User Offers: Typically offers a discount (e.g., 10-15% off) on the first purchase, often available via a code on the website.
Sale Events: Look out for sales during major Indian festivals (like Diwali), End of Season Sales (EOSS), and special occasions like Black Friday and their anniversary.
Combos: Frequently provides combo deals, such as "Buy 2 T-shirts for a special price."

What Sells More? (Popular Items)

The brand is most popular for its collection of Oversized T-shirts, which feature unique, subtle graphic prints and solid colors. Their cozy Hoodies and Joggers are also best-sellers, especially for their fabric quality and comfort.

Fastrack

India's leading youth fashion brand for watches, sunglasses, bags, and more.

What is Fastrack?

Fastrack is a prominent Indian fashion accessory brand from Titan Company. It targets the youth with a focus on trendy, affordable, and edgy designs. Initially known for watches, it has now expanded into a wide range of lifestyle products.

Main Product Categories

Watches: A vast collection of analog, digital, and chronograph watches for men and women.
Smart Wearables (Reflex): Fitness bands and smartwatches with features like activity tracking, heart rate monitoring, and notifications.
Sunglasses (Eyewear): Trendy sunglasses and frames in various styles.
Bags & Wallets: Backpacks, laptop bags, slings, and wallets designed for a young, urban audience.

Offers & Sale Events

New User Offers: Typically offers a discount (e.g., 10-15% off) on the first purchase via their official website or app.
Major Sales: Participates in End of Season Sales (EOSS) around June-July & Dec-Jan. Also features significant discounts during major Indian festive periods like Diwali, Holi, and Republic Day.
Brand-Specific Sales: Look out for "Fastrack Days" or anniversary sales for special promotions.

Loyalty Program (Titan Encircle)

Fastrack does not have a standalone premium membership. Instead, purchases are eligible for the Titan Encircle loyalty program. Customers earn points on every purchase which can be redeemed across Titan's brands (like Titan, Tanishq, Skinn). Members also get access to exclusive previews and offers.

Titan

A premier Indian lifestyle brand from the Tata Group, offering watches, jewelry, and more.

What is Titan?

Titan Company Limited is a leading Indian lifestyle company and a part of the Tata Group. It started as a watchmaker and has since diversified into various sectors, becoming a household name for quality and trust in India.

Key Brands & What Sells More

Watches: The original and iconic business. Brands include Titan (premium & formal), Fastrack (youth-focused, very popular), Sonata (value segment), and Nebula (solid gold watches).
Jewelry: This is their largest and most profitable segment. Brands include Tanishq (leading Indian jewelry brand), Mia by Tanishq (workwear jewelry), Zoya (luxury diamond jewelry), and CaratLane (omnichannel jewelry).
Eyewear: Titan Eyeplus is a major national chain for spectacles, sunglasses, and contact lenses.
Other Ventures: Skinn (fragrances) and Taneira (sarees and ethnic wear).

Major Sale Events

Festive Sales: Look for major discounts during Diwali, Dhanteras, and Akshaya Tritiya, especially for Tanishq.
End of Season Sales (EOSS): Typically held in January and July across most brands.
Brand-Specific Offers: Fastrack and other brands often run promotions targeting youth during college seasons or holidays.

Loyalty Program (Titan Encircle)

Membership: Free to join at any Titan store or website. It's a unified program across most Titan brands.
Benefits: Earn reward points on every purchase, get exclusive access to new collections and sales, and receive special birthday offers and vouchers.

New User Offers

On Titan's e-commerce websites (like Titan.co.in, Fastrack.in), new users who sign up for the newsletter often receive a welcome discount, typically 10-15% off their first purchase.

Lenskart

An omnichannel eyewear brand offering eyeglasses, sunglasses, and contact lenses.

What is Lenskart?

Lenskart is a leading Indian eyewear retailer that operates both online and through a vast network of physical stores. It provides a wide variety of stylish eyeglasses, premium sunglasses, and contact lenses, combining fashion with function.

Key Features & Services

Omnichannel Experience: Seamlessly browse online and try on or purchase in-store.
Home Try-On: Select up to 5 frames to try at home for free before making a purchase (service available in select cities).
3D Try-On: Use the camera on your phone or laptop to virtually see how frames look on your face.
Free Eye Exams: Certified optometrists conduct free, comprehensive eye check-ups at all Lenskart stores.

Lenskart Gold Membership

Core Benefit: The main perk is a continuous Buy One, Get One Free offer on almost all eyeglasses and sunglasses, including premium brands like Vincent Chase and John Jacobs.
Sharing: A single membership can be shared with friends and family by linking their mobile numbers.
Pricing: Membership plans vary in duration and price, often starting from around ₹600 + GST for 1 year.

Product Range & Offers

Lenskart is famous for its frequent sales and "Buy One, Get One" deals. The product range includes budget-friendly frames starting from ₹999 to premium designer eyewear, catering to all customer segments. They also offer specialized lenses like blue-light blocking, progressive, and transition lenses.

UNiDAYS

An exclusive discount platform providing free deals for college and university students.

What is UNiDAYS?

UNiDAYS is a global student discount network that connects students to exclusive offers from popular brands. It verifies student status to provide access to a wide range of deals, helping students save money on everyday expenses.

How It Works

Verification: Sign up and verify your student status for free using your university portal or student email address.
Access Deals: Once verified, you can browse and use hundreds of online and in-store discounts by generating unique codes or showing your UNiDAYS iD.

Featured Discount Categories

Technology: Special student pricing on laptops, phones, and accessories from brands like Apple, Samsung, and HP.
Fashion & Lifestyle: Percentage-off deals from top clothing, footwear, and beauty brands.
Food & Drink: Discounts at various restaurants and food delivery services.
Education & Travel: Savings on learning platforms, subscriptions, and travel bookings.

Pricing & Eligibility

Pricing: UNiDAYS is completely free for students to join and use. There are no hidden fees or pro versions.
Eligibility: You must be a student aged 16 or over, studying at a college or university, and able to verify your enrollment.
