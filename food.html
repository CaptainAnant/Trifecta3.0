Food & Beverages

Pizza Hut

An international pizza chain known for its pan pizzas, pasta, and iconic dining experience.

About Pizza Hut

Pizza Hut is one of the world's largest pizza chains, offering a wide variety of pizzas and other Italian-American dishes. In India, it is popular for its signature pan pizzas and a menu that includes both international classics and local, Indian-inspired flavours.

Menu Highlights

Pizza Types: Famous for its soft Pan Pizzas, with options like Stuffed Crust, Thin Crust, and classic hand-tossed bases.
Popular Flavours: Tandoori Paneer, Chicken Tikka, Veggie Supreme, Pepperoni.
Sides & More: Garlic Bread (Plain, Cheese, Supreme), a range of Pastas, Chicken Wings, and appetizers.
Desserts: The Choco Volcano cake is a widely popular dessert option.

Pricing & Average Cost

Starting Price: Personal pan pizzas typically start from approximately ₹200 - ₹250.
Average Cost for One: A satisfying meal for one person (e.g., a personal pizza and a beverage) usually costs between ₹350 - ₹500.

Popular Offers & Deals

Pizza Hut is known for its frequent promotions. Common deals include:
1 Plus 1 Deals: Get 2 personal or medium pizzas at a discounted price.
Buy One Get One (BOGO): Often available on specific days of the week for medium pizzas.
Meal Combos: Curated value meals for one, two, or a family/group.
Note: Offers are subject to change and may vary by location. Check the official app or website for current deals.

Domino’s

A leading pizza delivery chain in India famous for its fast service and popular deals.

What is Domino’s?

Domino's is one of the largest and most popular pizza delivery chains in India, operating thousands of stores across the country. It's known for its extensive delivery network and a menu tailored to Indian tastes.

Menu Highlights

Pizzas: A wide range of vegetarian and non-vegetarian options with various crusts like Classic Hand-Tossed, Cheese Burst, and Thin Crust.
Sides: Famous for Garlic Breadsticks, Stuffed Garlic Bread, Pastas, Tacos, and Chicken Wings.
Desserts & Beverages: Popular choices include the Choco Lava Cake, Brownie Fantasy, and a selection of cold drinks.

Offers & Average Cost

Common Deals: Look out for "Everyday Value Offers" (e.g., 2 regular pizzas starting at ₹99 each), app-exclusive coupons, and weekend BOGO (Buy One Get One) deals.
Starting Price: The budget-friendly Pizza Mania range starts from approximately ₹69.
Avg. Cost for One: A complete meal for one person (regular pizza + side) typically costs around ₹300 - ₹500.

Delivery & Service

Domino's is famous for its "30 minute delivery" promise, which aims to get your order to you quickly. They have a strong presence in most cities and towns, making them a reliable option for fast food delivery.

Burger King

Global fast-food chain famous for its flame-grilled burgers, especially the Whopper.

About Burger King

Burger King is an international chain of fast-food restaurants, renowned for its signature flame-grilled patties which give their burgers a distinct smoky flavor. In India, the menu is adapted to local tastes with a wide variety of vegetarian, chicken, and mutton options.

What It Serves

Burgers: Famous for the "Whopper" (available in Veg, Chicken, Mutton), along with other burger series like Crispy, Stunner, and King's Collection.
Sides: Serves classic sides including French Fries, Onion Rings, Chicken Wings, and Veggie Strips.
Beverages & Desserts: Offers a range of soft drinks, thick shakes, and desserts like sundaes and chocolate lava cake.

Pricing & Offers (India)

Starting Price: Basic items like the Crispy Veg Burger or small fries typically start from ₹70 - ₹90.
Average Cost (1 Person): A standard meal including a burger, medium fries, and a drink costs approximately ₹250 - ₹400.
Common Offers: Look for "King Deals" for value combos, app-exclusive discounts on their mobile app, and special offers on food delivery platforms like Zomato and Swiggy.

Good to Know

Burger King is a popular choice for quick, affordable meals. It is widely available for dine-in, takeaway, and delivery across major Indian cities through its own app and partnerships with food aggregators.

McDonald’s

Iconic global fast-food chain famous for its burgers, fries, and localized Indian menu.

About McDonald's India

McDonald's is a globally recognized fast-food leader. In India, it operates with a menu specifically tailored to local tastes, notably excluding beef and pork products and offering a wide array of popular vegetarian options.

Menu Highlights (India)

Food Types: Specializes in burgers, wraps, french fries, desserts, and beverages.
Vegetarian Range: Famous for items like the McAloo Tikki Burger, McVeggie, and McSpicy Paneer.
Non-Vegetarian Range: Includes the McChicken, McSpicy Chicken, Filet-O-Fish, and various chicken wraps.
Beverages & Desserts: Offers a range of sodas, coffees via McCafé, milkshakes, and desserts like the McFlurry.

Pricing & Offers

Starting Price: Entry-level options like the McAloo Tikki burger typically start from around ₹59.
Avg. Cost for One: A complete meal for one person usually costs between ₹200 - ₹350.
Offers & Deals: Regular discounts, BOGO (Buy One Get One) offers, and combo deals are available on the McDelivery app and in-store, including the popular McSaver menu.

Key Services

McDelivery: A robust home delivery service accessible via its dedicated app, website, and food delivery platforms.
McCafé: An in-house coffee shop in many outlets, offering a premium range of coffees, shakes, and pastries.

Chaayos

A modern tea cafe known for its customizable 'Meri Wali Chai' and Indian snacks.

What is Chaayos?

Chaayos is a popular Indian cafe chain that revolves around tea (chai). It offers a contemporary and relaxed environment to enjoy freshly made, customizable tea and a variety of complementary Indian snacks and quick bites.

What It Serves

Tea (Chai): Specializes in customizable "Meri Wali Chai" with 12 add-ons. Serves over 80,000 combinations, including Desi Chai, Kulhad Chai, and flavored options like Honey Ginger Lemon.
Snacks & Quick Bites: A curated menu of Indian street food such as Samosas, Vada Pav, Bun Maska, Maggi, and various Chaat items.
Meals: Select outlets offer light meals like Kulhad Matar Kulcha, Parathas, and homestyle meal bowls.
Other Beverages: Iced Teas, Lassis, Thick Shakes, and classic filter coffee.

Pricing & Average Cost

Starting Price: A basic cup of chai typically starts from ₹120 - ₹150.
Average Cost for One: A filling meal for one person, including a chai and a snack, costs approximately ₹300 - ₹450.
(Note: Prices are approximate and may vary by location and city.)

Common Offers & Deals

Chaayos frequently provides combo deals (e.g., Chai + Snack). They have a "LoyalTea" rewards program and often partner with food delivery platforms like Zomato and Swiggy for exclusive discounts and offers.

Biryani by Kilo

A premium delivery service for authentic, dum-cooked biryani served in traditional handis.

What is Biryani by Kilo?

Biryani by Kilo (BBK) is a popular food delivery chain that specializes in authentic, dum-cooked biryani. Their unique selling proposition is that each order is cooked fresh and delivered in the same sealed earthen pot (handi) it was cooked in, ensuring freshness and flavour.

Food Specialties

Biryani Types: Primarily serves three regional styles - Hyderabadi (spicy), Lucknowi (aromatic), and Kolkata (with potato and egg). Available in Veg, Chicken, and Mutton.
Other Dishes: The menu also includes a variety of Kebabs, Kormas (like Chicken Korma), and classic Indian breads (Rotis, Naans).
Desserts: Famous for traditional sweets like Phirni, which is often served in small clay pots (matkas).

Pricing & Offers

Starting Price: A 0.5 kg biryani handi, which is sufficient for one person, typically starts from around ₹400 - ₹500.
Average Cost for One: A complete meal for one person, including a biryani and a side, would cost approximately ₹500 - ₹700.
Common Offers: Look for discounts on their official website for direct orders. They also feature combo deals and are listed on platforms like Zomato and Swiggy, which may have their own offers.

Delivery & Presence

BBK operates in most major cities across India, including Bengaluru, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, and Hyderabad. As each order is cooked fresh, the standard delivery time is around 60-90 minutes. They also cater to larger gatherings with 1kg and 2kg handis.

EatClub

A members-only food club offering curated meals from popular in-house brands at a discount.

What is EatClub?

EatClub is a food delivery platform with a unique membership model. Instead of listing thousands of restaurants, it offers food from its own selection of popular, trusted cloud-kitchen brands. Membership is free, and it unlocks exclusive benefits for every order.

Featured Brands & Food Types

BOX8: Specializes in All-in-One Meals, Desi Boxes, and Wraps.
MOJO Pizza: Known for its pizzas with "2X Toppings".
Itminaan Biryani: Offers slow-cooked, dum-pukht biryanis.
GLOBO Ice Creams: A range of artisanal ice creams and desserts.
Mealful Rolls: Serves a variety of rolls and frankies.

Offers & Pricing

Key Offer: Members get a flat 30% discount on every order, always. No maximum limit.
Other Benefits: Free delivery, no surge pricing during peak hours, and exclusive access to new launches.
Average Cost (for one person): ₹250 - ₹450, before the member discount is applied.

Delivery & Service Areas

Average Delivery Time: 25-35 minutes.
Operating Cities: Currently serves major areas in Mumbai, Bangalore, Pune, Delhi-NCR, and Hyderabad.

Taco Bell

A popular fast-food chain serving a variety of Mexican-inspired foods like tacos and burritos.

What is Taco Bell?

Taco Bell is a globally recognized American fast-food chain that specializes in Mexican-inspired cuisine (Tex-Mex). It is known for its creative and often bold flavour combinations, catering to both vegetarian and non-vegetarian customers in India.

What It Serves

Food Types: The menu is built around core items like Tacos (crunchy and soft), Burritos (classic and grilled), Quesadillas, and Nachos.
Specialties: They also offer unique creations like the Chalupa, Crunchwrap, and various rice bowls.
Sides & Desserts: Common sides include Mexican Fries and Cheesy Nachos. Desserts feature items like Cinnamon Twists.

Pricing & Offers (Bengaluru)

Starting Price: Individual items on the value menu, like a basic taco, typically start from around ₹109.
Average Cost For One: A satisfying meal for one person, including a main item, side, and beverage, usually costs between ₹350 - ₹500.
Common Offers: Look for "Value Combos," limited-time product launches, and exclusive discounts on food delivery apps like Zomato and Swiggy.

Good to Know

Taco Bell is famous for its "Live Más" ("Live More") slogan. Most menu items are highly customizable, allowing you to add or remove ingredients like jalapeños, sour cream, and different sauces to suit your taste.

Subway

A global fast-food franchise specializing in customizable submarine sandwiches and salads.

What is Subway?

Subway is a popular quick-service restaurant known for its "build-your-own" approach to submarine sandwiches (subs) and salads. It allows customers to choose from a wide variety of breads, proteins, fresh vegetables, and sauces to create a personalized meal.

What It Serves

Food Types: The menu primarily consists of Subs (in 6-inch and footlong sizes), Salads, and Wraps. They offer a diverse range of both vegetarian (e.g., Paneer Tikka, Hara Bhara Kebab) and non-vegetarian (e.g., Chicken Teriyaki, Tuna) options.
Sides & Drinks: Common sides include cookies, chips, and hashbrowns, along with a selection of fountain and bottled beverages.

Pricing & Offers (India)

Starting Price: A basic 6-inch vegetarian sub or the "Sub of the Day" typically starts from ₹180 - ₹250.
Average Cost for One: A meal for one person, including a 6-inch sub, a drink, and a cookie, usually costs between ₹350 and ₹500.
Offers: The most popular deal is the "Sub of the Day," which features a specific sub at a discounted price each day of the week. Combo deals are also widely available.

Who is it for?

Subway is ideal for individuals seeking a quick, customizable, and relatively healthier fast-food meal compared to traditional fried options. It's a popular choice for students and office-goers for lunch.

KFC

A global fast-food chain famous for its signature pressure-fried chicken.

What is KFC?

Kentucky Fried Chicken (KFC) is one of the world's largest quick-service restaurant (QSR) chains, specializing in Original Recipe® fried chicken. Its menu in India is adapted to local tastes, offering both classic items and localized flavors.

What it Serves 🍗

Chicken Buckets: The signature offering with various combinations of fried chicken pieces.
Burgers & Wraps: Features the popular Zinger burger range and various wraps (twisters).
Snacks: Includes items like Hot Wings, Chicken Popcorn, and fries.
Localized Meals: Offers unique items for the Indian market like the Chicken & Rice Biryani Bowls.

Pricing & Offers 💰

Starting Price: Value meals and burgers typically start from around ₹150 - ₹200.
Average Cost (1 Person): A satisfying meal for one person usually costs between ₹300 - ₹500.
Common Offers: Look out for "Wednesday Specials" on buckets, app-exclusive deals, and various combo offers that provide better value for money.

How to Order 🛵

You can order through the official KFC India website or mobile app for delivery and takeaway. KFC is also widely available for delivery on major food aggregator platforms like Zomato and Swiggy.
