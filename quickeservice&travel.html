Quick Services

Swiggy

India's leading on-demand platform for food delivery, groceries, and package services.

What is Swiggy?

Swiggy is a comprehensive on-demand delivery platform in India. It started with restaurant food delivery and has expanded to include instant grocery delivery (Instamart), package pick-up and drop (Genie), and restaurant discovery (Dineout).

Key Service Areas

Food Delivery: Order from a vast network of restaurants, from local eateries to national chains, covering dozens of cuisines. Live order tracking is a standard feature.
Instamart: A quick commerce service delivering groceries, snacks, beverages, and home essentials in under 30 minutes.
Genie: A local courier service to send or receive packages (like documents, lunch boxes, or keys) across the city.
Dineout: Offers deals, discounts, and table booking at partner restaurants for dining-in experiences.

Swiggy One (Pro Version)

Benefits: The "Swiggy One" membership offers perks like free delivery on food orders above ₹149 and Instamart orders above ₹199, no surge fees, and extra discounts at partner restaurants.
Pricing (India): The price varies by user and location, but a typical 3-month plan costs between ₹700 - ₹1,500.

Service Details

Availability: Operates in over 500 cities across India.
Avg. Delivery Time: Food delivery is typically 25-40 minutes; Instamart aims for 15-30 minutes.
Avg. Cost for One (Food): A typical meal for one person can range from ₹250 - ₹400, including taxes and delivery fees for non-members.

Zomato

Your one-stop platform for restaurant discovery, food delivery, and dining out.

What is Zomato?

Zomato is a multinational restaurant aggregator and food delivery service. It allows users to search for restaurants, view menus and user reviews, order food for delivery, and book tables for dining out.

Food & Offerings

Cuisine Types: Serves a vast array of cuisines including North Indian, South Indian, Chinese, Italian, Continental, Fast Food, Desserts, and local street food.
Food Starting Price: Varies by city, typically starting from ₹100-₹150 for a single item from budget-friendly eateries.
Average Cost (1 Person): A complete meal usually costs between ₹250 - ₹500, depending on the restaurant and cuisine.
Common Offers: Frequent discounts like '50% off', new user offers, and partnerships with banks/payment wallets for additional savings.

Zomato Gold (Membership)

Benefits: Zomato's paid membership program offers benefits like free delivery on orders above a certain value, special discounts on both delivery and dining out, and priority service.
Pricing: The plan is typically offered in quarterly or annual subscriptions. A 3-month plan often costs around ₹999, but prices frequently change with promotional offers.

Service Details

Availability: Delivers in over 1000 cities across India, including all major metropolitan areas.
Average Delivery Time: Typically ranges from 20 to 45 minutes based on your location and restaurant prep time.
Ratings: Features a robust user-based rating system (out of 5) to help users choose quality restaurants.

Blinkit

An instant delivery service in India for groceries and essentials, delivered in minutes.

What is Blinkit?

Blinkit is an Indian quick commerce platform, owned by Zomato, that specializes in delivering groceries and a wide range of essential items to your doorstep in minutes. It operates through a network of local dark stores to ensure ultra-fast delivery times.

Key Offerings & Features

Delivery Time: The main feature is its speed, with most orders delivered within 10-20 minutes.
Product Range: Groceries, fruits & vegetables, snacks, beverages, personal care, home & cleaning items, and even some electronics and stationery.
Operating Hours: Typically operates from early morning (around 6 AM) to late night (past midnight), varying by city.

Fees & Charges

No Subscription: Blinkit does not have a "Pro" or "Prime" membership model. All users access the same service.
Delivery Fee: A nominal delivery fee is usually charged on orders, which can vary based on location, time of day, and order value.
Handling Charges: A small handling fee may be applied to orders below a certain minimum value. All charges are shown at checkout.

Availability & Use Cases

Cities: Available in major Indian cities including Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Bengaluru, Kolkata, Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai, and more.
Ideal For: Perfect for urgent needs, running out of an ingredient while cooking, late-night snack cravings, or quick daily essential top-ups without leaving home.

Zepto

Instant grocery and essentials delivery service, bringing items to your door in minutes.

What is Zepto?

Zepto is an Indian quick commerce company that delivers groceries and other essentials to customers within minutes. It operates through a network of "dark stores" or micro-warehouses located in neighborhoods to enable ultra-fast delivery times.

Key Features & Offerings

Delivery Time: Zepto's main feature is its speed, with a promised delivery time of 10-20 minutes from order placement.
Product Range: Offers over 5,000 products including fresh fruits & vegetables, dairy, snacks, beverages, cooking essentials, personal care items, and home cleaning supplies.
Operating Cities: Available in major Indian cities like Bengaluru, Mumbai, Delhi, Gurugram, Noida, Chennai, Hyderabad, Pune, and Kolkata.
Fees & Offers: Products are sold at or near MRP. Delivery fees are often waived for orders above a certain value (e.g., ₹199). Frequent discounts and new user offers are available.

How it Works

Customers place orders through the Zepto mobile app. The order is packed at the nearest dark store and assigned to a delivery partner, ensuring a quick and seamless process from click to delivery.

Who is it for?

Ideal for anyone needing last-minute groceries, forgotten items for a recipe, or daily essentials without wanting to step out. It caters to busy individuals, students, and families who value convenience and speed.

DMart Ready

DMart's online grocery service, offering a wide range of products at everyday low prices.

What is DMart Ready?

DMart Ready is the online grocery platform of the popular Indian supermarket chain, DMart. It allows customers to order groceries and household essentials online and have them delivered or pick them up from a designated point, carrying over DMart's core philosophy of "Everyday Low Prices" to the digital space.

Product Range & Service Model

Product Range: Offers a comprehensive selection of groceries, staples, fresh produce (in select locations), packaged foods, dairy products, personal care items, and household cleaning supplies.
Delivery Time: It is not an instant-delivery service. Deliveries are typically scheduled for the next day in specific time slots, making it ideal for planned purchases.
Service Options: Customers can choose between Home Delivery or self-pickup from a nearby DMart Ready Point, which are dedicated collection centers.

Pricing & Offers

Primary Offer: The main value proposition is consistently low prices across all products, rather than relying on frequent sales events or "flash" discounts.
Membership: There is no paid subscription or "pro" membership required to use the service.
New User Offers: Occasional promotions for new users may be available, but the focus remains on providing value for every order.

Serviceable Cities

Operates in major Indian cities including Mumbai, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Chennai, Pune, Ahmedabad, and the NCR region, among others. Availability is constantly expanding to new locations.
